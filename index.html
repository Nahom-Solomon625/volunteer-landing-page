FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to feel confident before your first shift.

Do I need experience to volunteer?

No. Most roles are beginner-friendly. You'll get a quick briefing, and an on-site lead will guide the team.

How do I know what to bring?

Each event listing includes a checklist. For most projects: comfortable shoes, water, and a positive attitude. We provide supplies.

Can I volunteer with friends or a group?

Yes. Grab spots together for an existing shift or request a custom group shift for teams of 8-80.

What about safety and accessibility?

We follow partner site policies, provide PPE when needed, and offer accessible roles whenever possible. Share any needs in the sign-up form.
